Our Story

Salmon HVAC was founded with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable HVAC services to Utah families at fair prices. For over four decades, we've stayed true to that mission, growing from a small family operation into one of the most trusted names in Utah's HVAC industry.

Based in Centerville, we understand the unique challenges of Utah's climate. From the bitter cold of winter inversions to the scorching heat of summer afternoons, we've seen it all—and we've helped thousands of homeowners and businesses stay comfortable through it all.

As a family-owned business, we treat every customer like a neighbor. That means honest assessments, fair prices, and work that's done right the first time.
