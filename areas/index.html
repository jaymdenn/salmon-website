HVAC Services Across Northern Utah

From our Centerville headquarters, Salmon HVAC serves Davis, Weber, Salt Lake, and Morgan counties. Find your city below — same trusted team, same 46-year commitment wherever you are.

Salmon HVAC has been servicing homes and businesses across northern Utah since 1979. What started as a local Centerville operation has grown to serve communities from Morgan County's mountain valleys to Salt Lake City's downtown commercial district — all from the same family-owned shop on Main Street. Whether you're in a 1940s Bountiful bungalow without central AC or a brand-new Daybreak home looking for heat pump optimization, we know the specific challenges your home faces.
