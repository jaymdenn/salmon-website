HVAC Services in Northern Utah

Residential and commercial heating, cooling, and air quality services across Davis, Weber, Salt Lake, and Morgan counties — from Salmon HVAC, Northern Utah's trusted contractor since 1979.

Salmon HVAC — legally Salmon Plumbing & Heating Co., Inc. — has served Northern Utah homeowners and businesses since 1979. As a Daikin Comfort Pro Authorized Dealer, we provide the full range of residential and commercial HVAC services, from same-day repairs to new system installations and commercial VRF projects. Whether you need a furnace fixed before tonight or a VRF system engineered for a new medical office, we have the expertise and equipment to handle it.

Utah's climate is unforgiving on both ends. Wasatch Front summers regularly exceed 100°F, and winter lows in Davis and Weber County frequently drop into the single digits — with Morgan County seeing temperatures well below zero. Every service we offer is designed around these real-world conditions, not generic HVAC approaches developed for milder climates.

Why Utah Homeowners and Businesses Choose Salmon HVAC

There's no shortage of HVAC contractors in Davis and Weber County. Here's why customers have chosen Salmon HVAC since 1979:

  • 46 years of continuous operation in Northern Utah — We've been in the same community, serving the same area, for 46 years. We understand how Utah's altitude, alkaline water, desert heat, and cold winters affect HVAC equipment in ways that national chains or recently arrived contractors simply don't.
  • Daikin Comfort Pro Authorized Dealer — The top tier of Daikin's dealer program, requiring ongoing factory training, installation quality standards, and access to Daikin's extended warranty programs. We're not just any dealer — we're Daikin's authorized representative for this region.
  • Manual J load calculations on every installation — We never size a system based on square footage alone. Proper load calculations account for insulation, windows, ceiling height, sun exposure, and Utah's specific climate data.
  • No overtime charges on weekday service calls — A furnace failure at 8 PM on a Thursday costs the same as a 9 AM call. This is our policy and we don't waiver from it on weekday calls.
  • Upfront pricing, always — We give you a firm price before any work begins. No surprise fees on the invoice.
  • Honest repair vs. replace guidance — We tell you what we would do if it were our own home. Sometimes that's a repair. Sometimes that's a replacement. We're not pushing you either way — we're giving you the information to make the best decision for your situation.
