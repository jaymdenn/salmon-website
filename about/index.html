About Salmon HVAC — 46 Years of Trusted Service

Family-owned since 1979. Rooted in Centerville. Committed to every customer across Northern Utah.

Our Story

Salmon HVAC has been keeping Northern Utah families and businesses comfortable since 1979. Founded in Centerville, Utah by the Salmon family, the company began as a small heating and plumbing operation serving Davis County neighbors who needed reliable, honest work at a fair price. That same commitment drives us today.

Over four-plus decades, we've grown from a modest local outfit into one of the most trusted HVAC contractors in the region — but we've never lost the small-business values that built our reputation. We know our customers by name. We show up when we say we will. We tell you exactly what your system needs, and nothing it doesn't.

Today, Salmon HVAC serves homeowners and commercial clients across Davis, Weber, Salt Lake, and Morgan counties. We install, repair, and maintain the full range of HVAC equipment — from standard central air and gas furnaces to modern heat pumps, ductless mini-splits, and large-scale VRF systems for commercial buildings.

As a Daikin Comfort Pro Authorized Dealer, we carry and install Daikin's industry-leading equipment backed by some of the strongest manufacturer warranties available. Our technicians receive ongoing factory training so they stay current with the latest technology and best practices.
