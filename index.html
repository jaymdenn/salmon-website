Our Services

Residential Service

Keep your home comfortable all year despite Utah's extreme climate—10°F winters to 95°F+ summers. We handle AC tune-ups, furnace repairs, heat pumps, ductless mini-splits, and full system replacements for all major brands. We also service water heaters and specialize in tankless installs for Utah's growing homes.

Commercial Services

Custom HVAC solutions for Utah offices, retail, and industrial spaces — including VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow) system installation, maintenance, and repair. We service multi-zone commercial systems built for our high-altitude climate and extreme temperature swings.

$85 Diagnostic

Not sure what's wrong? Our $85 diagnostic service identifies issues fast—from furnaces struggling in Utah's extreme cold to ACs freezing in our dry climate. Get back to comfort quickly with expert troubleshooting for all heating, cooling, and water heater concerns.
